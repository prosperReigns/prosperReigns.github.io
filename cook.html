To celebrate creativity and affordability in food, FOODSURPLUS is introducing the “Cook and Win Challenge.”

Participants use the N5,000 pack to create unique family meals, record or photograph their dish, and win prizes including:

  • Free food bundles for a month
  • Branded kitchenware
  • Cash rewards for the best family meals

The campaign encourages dignity in low-budget cooking while building community.
