FOODSURPLUS INDEPENDENT BUSINESS OWNER (FIBO)

FIBOs (FOODSURPLUS Independent Business Owners) are freelance marketers who sell FOODSURPLUS products without owning a physical shop. They operate independently, reaching customers in their communities, workplaces, churches, online platforms, and more.

Why Become a FIBO?

  • 1 Register FREE to join the FOODSURPLUS movement
  • 2 Earn 10% profit on every product purchased
  • 3 Buy at distributor discount and resell at full retail price
  • 4 Flexible, low-risk business you can run from anywhere
  • 5 Meet real food needs with products people are already looking for

Requirements to Start:

  • Free registration as a FIBO member
  • Minimum purchase of ₦50,000 worth of FOODSURPLUS products per order • (This could be multiple ₦5000 combo packs or other items)
  • Willingness to market and serve customers around your area

What FIBOs Do:

  • Promote and sell FOODSURPLUS ₦5000 Combo Packs and other food items
  • Purchase at 10% discount and keep the margin as profit
  • Take orders from friends, family, estates, or online communities
  • Deliver or coordinate pick-up for customers
  • Join our WhatsApp group for updates, support, and marketing tips whatsapp

How to Register:

  • Fill out the FIBO registration form below
  • Join our FIBO WhatsApp group for guidance and product updates whatsapp
  • Make your first ₦50,000 order and begin earning immediately
