Our Story

FOODSURPLUS was born from a simple yet powerful insight: too many Nigerians go hungry while food rots in surplus at the farm level or in warehouses. Our CEO, Chibuogwu Benson-Oraelosi, once watched a widow in a Lagos market struggle to afford yams while the same produce was being wasted in Benue.
